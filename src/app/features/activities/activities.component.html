Fun & Learning Activities 🎨

At Nanhe Munne Play Center, we believe in learning through play. Our daily schedule includes activities that boost creativity, communication, problem-solving, and Islamic values — all wrapped in fun!

🧩 Puzzle Time

Enhances logical thinking and motor skills. From animal shapes to Arabic letters, kids solve puzzles that entertain and educate.

🎲 Islamic Board Games

Kids enjoy custom games based on Prophets’ stories, daily duas, and good manners — all while building teamwork and memory skills.

🎨 Arts & Crafts

From painting Eid cards to crafting Kaaba models, we let our little artists express their imagination while learning about Deen.

🎶 Nasheed Time

Gentle, uplifting nasheeds are played and sung by kids in group time. It’s a calming and joyful session that promotes listening and rhythm.

🏃‍♂️ Physical Play

Outdoor games, hopscotch, mini-obstacle courses — kids stay active, burn energy, and build coordination. Every move matters!

🧠 Memory & Matching Games

These card-based games help sharpen memory using Islamic themes — match the name to the Prophet or the dua to the occasion!

✨ Our goal is to make every moment joyful and meaningful, inside and outside the classroom!
